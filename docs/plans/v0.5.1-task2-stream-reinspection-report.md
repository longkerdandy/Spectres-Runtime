the Runtime locally and sent two streaming requests to
`POST /agents/recipe/runs?stream=true`:

1. A **detail** request: "番茄炒蛋怎么做" → expected `search_recipes` then
   `get_recipe_detail`.
2. A **search-only** request: "晚饭想吃点清淡的，推荐一道汤" → expected
   `search_recipes` only.

Recorded all SSE events to JSONL and analyzed event types, tool-call payloads,
and final metrics.

## 4. Reasoning Content

No `ReasoningStarted`, `ReasoningStep`, or `ReasoningCompleted` events appear.
Reasoning is surfaced as **flat `reasoning_content` strings** inside `RunContent`
deltas, identical to Task 1.

For the detail request:

- `RunContent` events carry `reasoning_content` during the model's thinking
  phase, then switch to `content` for the final answer.
- The final `RunCompleted` also contains a full `reasoning_content` string.

This does **not** match the Web plan's assumption of `message.extra_data.reasoning_steps`
with structured `title`, `result`, `confidence`, and `next_action`. To provide
structured steps, the Runtime must add `ReasoningTools` (§3 of the v0.5.1 plan).
